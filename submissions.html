Project/Sample Submissions

Contact the IMR Manager directly to initiate your project's consultation, then download our Excel form:

Amplicon Sample Submission Form

This form is to be filled out with all of your details for tracking purposes and has sample preparation and shipping guidelines (please pay careful attention for international clients). You will be given a unique project/sample identifier in the form of "IMR-YYYYMM-00" which will allow sample tracking, will show up on your billing information and be listed in the queue at right.

Sample queuing and real-time status

In order to offer as flexible (and economical) a service as possible to the community, we will not be insisting that clients fulfill a complete multiplexed run of 380 (380 + 4 negatives = 384) samples. We offer the option of sequencing less samples, but with the caveat that your samples may take a bit longer in the pipeline as we wait for the remainder of the 380 slots to be filled by other projects/individuals. Of course, you will always have the option of expediting your service by purchasing the complete run at any time (even with less than 380 samples).

We will attempt to maintain as close to real-time as possible project/sample information on the queue display on the right of this page. This will allow you to monitor the progress of your samples through the IMR workflow. It will also allow prospective clients to get a sense of how full the current queued sequencing run is for those who wish to multiplex without contributing a whole run of 380 (as discussed above).
