Project/Sample Submissions

Coming soon we will have here more info on the procedures for project and sample submissions, including a PDF form to be filled out with all of your details for tracking purposes. You will be given a unique project identifier in the form of "IMR-YYYYMM-00" which will allow sample tracking and ease of communication between the IMR and clients. In the meantime, please contact the IMR Manager directly to discuss project submissions.

Sample queuing and real-time status

In order to offer as flexible (and economical) a service as possible to the community, we will not be insisting that clients fulfill a complete multiplexed run of 95 (95 + 1 negative control = 96) or 380 (380 + 4 negatives = 384) samples. We will offer the option of sequencing less samples, but with the caveat that your samples may take a bit longer in the pipeline as we wait for the remainder of the 95 or 380 slots to be filled by other projects/individuals. Of course, you will always have the option of expediting your service by purchasing the complete run at any time (even with less than 95/380 samples).

We will attempt to maintain as close to real-time as possible project/sample information on the queue display on the right of this page. This will allow you to monitor the progress of your samples through the IMR workflow. It will also allow prospective clients to get a sense of how full the current queued sequencing run is for those who wish to multiplex without contributing a whole run of 95/380 (as discussed above).
