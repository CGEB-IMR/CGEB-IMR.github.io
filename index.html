Welcome to the CGEB IMR.

A sequencing and bioinformatic analysis resource for the Dalhousie University community. We help researchers,
clinicians and industry tackle the challenges of microbiome science, guiding them from study design consultation,
through sample preparation and sequencing, to final data analysis.
