Real-Time Sample Queue

Real-time project/sample information is available in the queue display below. This allows you to monitor the progress of your samples through the IMR workflow. In order to offer as flexible (and economical) a service as possible to the community, clients do not have to fill complete runs. The main way sequencing is done is that your samples are mixed with other clients - this may take a bit longer (primarily for NextSeq/PacBio runs - MiSeq runs are always full) in the pipeline as we wait for the remainder of the run slots to be filled by other projects/individuals. Dalhousie clients (since internal) and Commercial clients (since they help subsidize for academics by paying a premium) receive queue priority, but for external academics, in cases of important deadlines, there are some options for expediting your service (contact us).
